Coding 5k Challenge

The Coding 5k Challenge will increase participation in coding camps and classes at Public Libraries, Community Centers, and schools to 5,000 youth a year, particularly in underrepresented neighborhoods.
As part of the San José Public Library and the City of San José’s commitment to increasing the availability of quality STEM programming to Silicon Valley teens, the Coding 5k Challenge will increase access to coding focused programs as well as deepen the quality of the programming through the use of both assessment tools and expert volunteers.
In the capital of Silicon Valley, our children should be able to participate in this vibrant tech industry. We are proud to offer coding experiences that inspire San José’s students to learn, think, and grow in new ways. Whether a child’s future includes a career in computer science or not, coding experiences expose them to new learning opportunities and build skills that reinforce academic success, such as creativity, risk-taking, collaboration, and learning as a process rather than a product.
