Service Gen2GenSJ

Gen2GenSJ

Gen2Gen connects adults 50 years and older with opportunities to work youth from underserved opportunities for for service opportunities.
Established Gen2Gen in 2017, Gen2Gen helps adults 50 years and older to find youth-serving opportunities, empower their spirit of service, unleash their creativity, and leverage their professional expertise to make a difference in the lives of San José youth in underserved communities.
For more information go to the Generation to Generation site.
